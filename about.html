About

Soulful Bakers goal to is promote a healthy through baking. Baking can be used to create healthy meals and delicious treats which are good for the soul. Baking can be used as a form of meditation and can provide a calm hobby in a busy lifestyle. Our group has many classes (Possibly Link This) which will help promote the benefits of baking.

Our main location is 4 Baker St. Hung Hom, Hong Kong which is just a 5-minute walk away from Whampoa Station. We have a passion for baking, and we hope you will too.
