Meal Prep:

This class will teach you the wonders of meal prepping, and how it allows you to premake your weeks meals. This method will help keep your diet on track as you have healthy meals on hand. It will also be cheaper in the long run. As you will be buying in bulk, and it will lower the temptation for ordering take-out. In class we will give you some meal ideas, and help you structure your weekly diet. Please bring your own tupperware, but we will have some spare.

Pepare Yoga:

Weeks can be stressful, this class will be based on a calming yoga routine, which will relax the body and mind in preperation for the upcomming week. Each week we will try to do a different method of yoga relaxation with a different yoga instructor. All level of skills are welcome, as this will be a relaxing experience with different levels skills required. Please bring your own yoga mat and a towl and we will notify you if any other equipment is needed.
